Barack Obama by God Alosson

Character Data:
Name: Barack Obama
Origin: Hawaii
Likes: Being president.
Dislikes: Unknown
Friends: People from the white house.

Why I made this:
There are no Obamas for MUGEN, this is considered the first version for MUGEN.

Background:
This character is a parody to alot of other characters and creators.

Ingame:
Being a sprite edit, he plays just like Omega Tom Hanks, but he attacks you with random crap and not
movies and he is hittable, he can also summon Dio Brando to crush you or summon 
a Shrimp bus to kill you.

Moveset:
X/Y/Z/A/B/C:
Random Crap

Start:
Summon Dio Brando (Requires 1 power level)
Dio's carelessness causes him to damage everyone reguardless of who summoned him.

X+Y/Y+Z/Z+X:
Bus of Doom (requires 1 and a half power levels)
Your classic OTW Bus of doom move, this move is now killer and will kill anyone it 
touches in almost a single hit, also good as a crowd control move.

A+X/B+Y/C+Z:
Apocalyse Head (1 or more levels to use)
A replacement for Hooch, this is an OHKO move.

~D,F,D,F, x+z:
Armageddon (1 and a half levels to use)
Again, Discord most be a big fan of OTW, because now hes copying another one of his
moves, you would know what this does.

~x, x, F, a, b
Brutal Murder
Another OHKO Move, this will make you shiz your pants.

~D,F,D,F, b+c
Unexplainable White Screen
Unexplainable move.

~D,F,D,F, y+z:
Apocalyse Storm
Barack summons a storm of Apocalyse heads to kill you!

Facing a cheap character:
Anti-cheap mode
Unhittable becomes un-hittable when facing someone like Rare Akuma, so don't try fighting
him with a cheap character.

This was made by God Alosson, with bits and pieces of code from other characters.
